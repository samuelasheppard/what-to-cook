What to cook...

Samuel Sheppard 2022

Look what you have in your fridge, pantry or freezer and enter it in to the search bar below. If you make a mistake, click the item to remove it. Once you click search, it will display a list of available recipes you can cook.

Alternatively, click the "Random Recipe" button to be given a completely random recipe.

If you're hungry for humour then ask for a random joke instead...
